Trip planning

Things to do in Rishikesh without rushing the city.

Build a trip around yoga mornings, river time, aarti evenings, local food, and one or two bigger adventures.

Best first-time experiences

Rishikesh works best when your days have space. Keep one anchor activity each day, then leave room for cafes, river walks, and quiet time near your homestay.

River rafting

Popular with adventure travellers. Choose licensed operators, confirm current river conditions, and avoid booking during unsafe weather.

Yoga and meditation

Stay near Tapovan, Swarg Ashram, or Muni Ki Reti if daily classes and quieter mornings matter.

Ganga Aarti

Plan an evening at Triveni Ghat or Parmarth Niketan and arrive early for a calmer spot.

One-day easy itinerary

  1. Start with sunrise tea and a riverside walk.
  2. Take a morning yoga class or visit an ashram area.
  3. Have lunch in Tapovan or near Muni Ki Reti.
  4. Visit a waterfall, bridge area, or market depending on energy.
  5. End with Ganga Aarti and a simple dinner near your stay.

Good questions before booking activities

  • Is pickup included from your homestay area?
  • What is the current safety guidance for rafting or waterfall walks?
  • Does the activity suit children, seniors, or non-swimmers?
  • What should you carry, and what should you avoid carrying?
