Sacred riverfront

Triveni Ghat: evening aarti, river steps, and local Rishikesh life.

One of the most important riverfront places in Rishikesh, especially for travellers who want to experience the Ganga Aarti respectfully.

Why visit Triveni Ghat

Triveni Ghat is a central Rishikesh riverfront known for ritual bathing, evening Ganga Aarti, nearby temples, and local markets. It is a meaningful stop for pilgrims and a memorable evening experience for first-time visitors.

How to plan the visit

  • Arrive before sunset to find a comfortable place without rushing.
  • Dress modestly and keep the steps clear for worshippers.
  • Carry only essentials; evenings can become busy.
  • Avoid putting plastic, flowers, or packaging into the river.
  • Check with your host for the current aarti timing before leaving.

Where to stay nearby

Choose Triveni Ghat or central Rishikesh if you want easy access to aarti, markets, transport, and practical city conveniences. Choose Tapovan or Swarg Ashram if cafes, yoga, and quieter walking routes matter more.
