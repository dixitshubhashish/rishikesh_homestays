City overview

About Rishikesh for travellers choosing where to stay.

Rishikesh is a pilgrimage town, yoga destination, river city, and adventure base. Picking the right area can change the whole trip.

What Rishikesh is known for

Rishikesh sits by the Ganga in Uttarakhand and is widely known for yoga, ashrams, temples, Ganga Aarti, rafting, cafes, and access to the Garhwal Himalayas. It is also an important starting point for many Uttarakhand pilgrimage routes.

Where to stay

Tapovan

Best for cafes, yoga studios, rafting access, and travellers who want an active base.

Swarg Ashram

Good for slower days, ashram walks, spiritual stays, and being closer to classic Rishikesh atmosphere.

Triveni Ghat

Useful for families, markets, aarti evenings, railway-road access, and practical city movement.

Traveller tips

  • Many visitors prefer vegetarian food while staying in the main Rishikesh area.
  • Ask your homestay about stairs, parking, luggage help, and vehicle access before arrival.
  • Traffic can slow down near bridges and popular ghats during evenings, weekends, and holidays.
  • For quiet sleep, ask whether the stay is near a main road, cafe strip, temple loudspeaker, or event area.
